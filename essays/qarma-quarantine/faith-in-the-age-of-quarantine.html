Faith in The Age of Quarantine


This one’s from The WhatsApp University. And Thank God for His Small Mercy.

09 March 2020

Shubhrangshu Roy

Mecca & Medina are out of bounds ...

Vatican’s just shut shop...

Tirupati & Shirdi have downed shutters long before Time arrived ...

Our created gods have closed their doors to us Humans ...

But...

Our unmade gods have been working round the clock...

So, while our mandirs, masjids, churches remain in pushback mode, our doctors and hospitals (our real gods and temples) wait for us to walk right in, right now, should we reveal the symptoms of getting scarred.

So, if and when you are cured, do not forget to thank God Almighty, as you spoil for another fight with your doc for billing you high!
