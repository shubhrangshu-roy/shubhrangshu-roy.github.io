Story of the Virus


26 April 2021

Shubhrangshu Roy

In the Hindu, I am the Hindu virus
In the Muslim, Muslim
In the Christian, I am the Christian virus
In the Jew, Jewish
In the Sikh, I am the Sikh virus
In the Buddhist, Buddhist
In the Shaman, I am the Shaman virus
In the Atheist, Atheist
Call Me Whatever You Will
Unseen, Unheard, Untouched
I Am The One And The Only
