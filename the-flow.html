The Flow

Reimagining Vedic wisdom, one step at a time, one day at a time.

The Flow is a daily mental ritual that compresses the immensity of India’s timeless Vedic philosophy through a daily step across 365 days of the calendar. Crafted by India’s faceless seers, it charts the course of the soul’s existential journey from here and now to the source of all creation of this Universe. Join me on this eventful walk through time and distance to discover Who am I.
